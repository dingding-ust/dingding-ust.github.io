Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in the fields of Deep Generative Models and Statistical Machine Learning. I am particularly fascinated by the capabilities and theoretical underpinnings of Diffusion Models.

I am keen on exploring how these models can be improved in terms of efficiency, controllability, and applicability to various scientific and creative domains. Understanding the statistical properties and limitations of such complex models is another core aspect of my research focus.
